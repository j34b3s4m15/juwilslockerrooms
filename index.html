About Me

Growing up, seeing different colors inspired me to be an artist. Although some days may be mundane, it never stopped me from seeing the good things in life. Being an artist means being understandable of emotions and also facing burnouts.

Albeit not really starting as a good watercolor and marker user at first, I learned so much via books and experiences. Majority of my inspirations came from range of retro drawings, colorful doodles, impressionism/post-impressionism, and old-school anime.

Oh, and sometimes, I do some pixel art and other digital animations, which are what I am currently trying to apply to some of my stuff.

My Principles

There are so many ways to consider when making art, such as how you start and how you end. At the process, never leave it as it is if you feel like there's something good to add on the concept.

Making art is like building a structure, you first need to figure out its mould first before the final appearance or aesthetic.
